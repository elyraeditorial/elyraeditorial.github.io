What is ELYRA?

ELYRA is an independent fashion & culture publication documenting identity, presence, and creative expression through editorial storytelling. As a publication, ELYRA is inclusive of all genders and welcomes contributors across fashion, beauty, design, and culture.

Relationship to Glamour Society

ELYRA is a publication. Glamour Society Modeling Agency is a women-exclusive representation and development agency. Editorial features may include contributors of any gender; representation is handled separately.
